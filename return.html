Returns & Refunds Policy

Returns & Refunds We accept returns under specific conditions to ensure fairness and quality. Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unused, unwashed, and in its original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Items that show signs of wear, damage caused by the customer, or altered products are not eligible for return. Once a return is approved and received, refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. Return shipping costs may be the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect. To initiate a return, please contact us with your order details through the Contact page.
