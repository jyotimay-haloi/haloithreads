Shipping & Delivery Policy

Shipping & Delivery We aim to process and deliver every order with care and clarity. Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after confirmation. Once dispatched, delivery usually takes 5–8 business days, depending on the destination and courier partner. Shipping is handled by trusted third-party logistics providers. Tracking details will be shared once the order has been dispatched. Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to factors beyond our control, including courier delays, weather conditions, or regional restrictions. If you experience any issues with your delivery, please reach out through our Contact page for assistance.
